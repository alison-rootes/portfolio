My Life at Home

Outside of school I love to hang out with my friends and boyfriend. I also really enjoy spending time with my family and pets. My Work

Noblesville Schools Mascott

Currently, I work at North Elementary school in Noblesville, IN. I am an Instructional assistant in an FAP special Education classroom and I LOVE IT! I love working with the kids and helping them work through their problems. It is frustrating sometimes, but that is a perk of working with kiddos. In all, they help me keep my creativity, and my sanity on its toes.
